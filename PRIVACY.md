# Privacy Policy for FluxOrigin

**Last updated: December 04, 2025**

## 1. Data Collection and Usage
FluxOrigin ("the Application") is designed to prioritize user privacy. The Application operates locally on your device. We **do not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information, documents, or translation data with the developer or any third-party servers.

## 2. Local AI Processing
All translation tasks are performed locally on your computer using Local LLMs (Large Language Models) via Ollama. Your documents and data never leave your device.

## 3. Internet Permissions
The Application requests internet access permissions solely for the purposes of: checking for application updates, downloading necessary AI models, and retrieving external data to assist in generating translation dictionaries. These actions are performed only at the user's explicit request. No personal user data is transmitted during these processes.
